About AI Plagiarism Checker

This project is a solo creation born from a personal need to validate content authenticity with clarity and confidence. I’ve worked diligently to ensure the platform is both powerful and easy to use, letting you focus on your writing while I handle the verification behind the scenes.

Built entirely with open-source tools and custom logic, this project represents hours of dedication toward solving a real-world problem. With every line of code, the intention was to craft a safe, reliable, and efficient checker that anyone—student, researcher, or writer—can trust.

Mission

To build a responsible tool that empowers users to protect their original content from misuse.

Innovation

Using state-of-the-art NLP models like BERT to analyze content deeply, beyond surface-level checks.

Privacy

User trust is everything. No content is stored, tracked, or shared. What you upload stays yours.
